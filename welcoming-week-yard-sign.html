Welcoming Week 2022!

About Welcoming Falls Church

Welcoming Falls Church is building a community of neighbors. Our goals are hospitality toward immigrants and refugees and opportunity for us all. We believe that by engaging locally we can overcome divisions and reinforce some of our society's greatest strengths. Inspired by a national movement called Welcoming America, we are grassroots, nonpartisan, and public-private in character. We invite you to learn more and to join us.

About Welcoming Week

As you may know, Welcoming Week, this year September 9-18, is Welcoming America's (and Welcoming Falls Church's) biggest annual event. Learn about our Welcoming Week activities here.

Welcoming Week Community Food Drive

One of our annual Welcoming Week activities is a Community Food Drive. The Food Drive encourages all Falls Church residents to come together to serve those in need. The Food Drive will begin on September 11 and continue through September 16.

The Food Drive benefits Spend Yourself Food Pantry, operated by our partner Columbia Baptist Church, which has a well-established presence on Glen Carlyn Road in Seven Corners.

The most needed items for the Food Drive are:

  • Canned Fruit (100% Juice)
  • Canned Meat (chicken, tuna and salmon)
  • Dried Beans-1 or 2lb bags
  • Hearty Soup (Progresso, Healthy Choice, Chunky Soup)
  • Macaroni and Cheese
  • No Sodium Added Canned Corn
  • No Sodium Added Canned Tomatoes
  • Non-Instant Oatmeal
  • Non-sweetened Cereal
  • Pasta
  • Pasta Sauce
  • White Rice-1 or 2lb bags
  • Feminine Hygiene Supplies (individually wrapped pads and panty liners)

You can drop off these items at our Ice Cream Social, as well as locations around Falls Church throughout Welcoming Week.

Thanks for your support and we look forward to an extremely successful drive. If you have any questions, please email Steve Mostow at smostow@yahoo.com (subject line: Food Drive).
