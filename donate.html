Get Involved

We look forward to meeting you! Please email us for information about activities like our Community Thanksgiving celebration, to get involved in planning or to attend or for information about volunteering in our Adult & Family Literacy Center or in other programs.

We also welcome financial support. Welcoming Falls Church Inc. is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization operating with Tax ID 88-2015871.

Please consider helping us with a donation to serve as foundation for all of our programming. We appreciate gifts of major donors, as well as friends who would support us on a monthly basis whether $25, $50 or $100 a month. Donate here.

Your gifts help to support programming like the following:

Adult and Family Literacy Center

Launched in spring 2022, the Adult & Family Literacy Center is focused on helping immigrants and refugees gain the literacy and language skills they need to thrive in our community. Children of adult learners can participate in the Children's Literacy Activities Program (CLAP!). Classes are held two nights a week during the fall and spring at Oak Street Elementary School, with the support of Falls Church City Public Schools.

Community Building Activities

Committed to creating a community of neighbors who are welcoming of immigrants and refugees, Welcoming Falls Church hosts annual events like our Welcoming Week Ice Cream Social and Thanksgiving dinners for recently arrived immigrants and resettled refugees. Intended to raise awareness and foster relationships within our community, these events are free for community members.

Checks can be made out to Welcoming Falls Church and mailed to Emily Draper, Treasurer, Welcoming Falls Church, 122 West Greenway Boulevard, Falls Church, VA 22046; or donate online here.
